About us

We are a group of English language teachers with master's degrees in Teaching English as a Foreign/Second Language, with international certifications for teaching English, with a proficiendy of English level at the C1 level of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages and certified by Cambridge ESOL Examinations, and with more than 10 years of experience in teaching English.

Some of us are native speakers of North American, others of British English, and some are native speakers of Spanish, but all of us with a C1 (= advanced) level of language proficiency and with all the credentials mentioned in the previous paragraph in terms of university education (master's degree) and international certifications.

We started our online school during the time of the COVID-19 pandemic in June 2020. We did so with the goal of offering affordable English courses no matter where and when our students wish to learn this language of global communication, and thus, contribute to the reduction of social inequalities that emerge when people do not have access to quality education.
